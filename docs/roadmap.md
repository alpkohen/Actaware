# ActAware — product roadmap (sources & scope)

Internal reference; public page: `roadmap.html` on the live site.

Last updated: March 2026.

## What we cover today

17 official UK feeds (GOV.UK branches, HMRC, Home Office, DWP, ICO, EHRC, Acas, TPR, HSE, Legislation.gov.uk, Employment Tribunal decisions, FWA-related pages, etc.). Output is plain-English employer briefings — not legal advice. No guarantee of completeness (see site disclaimer).

## Planned source expansion (priority)

1. **EAT** — appellate employment decisions; complements existing ET monitoring.
2. **DBS** — health, education, care employers.
3. **CQC / sector regulators** — phased, regulated sectors.
4. **CAC / Certification Officer** — if demand from union-recognition-heavy employers.
5. **Scotland / Wales** — selective devolved official feeds where relevant.
6. **FWA** — keep aligned as GLAA and related functions consolidate under FWA per government timeline.

## Not in scope (this product)

FCA / SM&CR and similar financial-conduct regimes — separate product if ever.

## Prioritisation

Customer demand, deliverable employer value, legal/terms for each feed.
